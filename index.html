📄 About This Research

Research Overview

Full context — objectives, methodology, key concepts and acronyms from Jafali Adam's final-year proposal.

📌 Research Details

Title: Assessing Opportunities for Integrating Forecast-Based Financing into Malawi's Disaster Risk Financing Framework

Researcher: Jafali Adam (DRM/005/21)

Institution: Ndata School of Climate & Earth Sciences, MUST

Supervisor: Mrs. T. Nthara

Timeline: December 2025 – May 2026

Budget: MWK 210,000 | Location: National scope, Blantyre-based

🎯 Research Objectives

  • 1Examine existing disaster risk financing policies and institutional arrangements in Malawi
  • 2Assess the level of institutional and technical capacity among key stakeholders for implementing FbF
  • 3Evaluate the existing protection gap and financial impacts of climate-related disasters in Malawi

🔬 Methodology

Design: Mixed methods — qualitative and quantitative

Qualitative: ~10 Key Informant Interviews from DoDMA, humanitarian partners, insurance/banking, and academia. Document review of DRF Strategy (2019–2030) and DRM Act (2015).

Quantitative: Secondary data from EM-DAT, PDNAs, and World Bank to calculate the protection gap.

Analysis: Thematic analysis + Excel/SPSS descriptive statistics.
